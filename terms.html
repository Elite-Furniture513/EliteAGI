1. Layaway – 90‑Day Limit

  1. Timeframe & Forfeiture – Layaway balances must be paid in full and picked up or delivered within 90 calendar days of deposit. After that, all funds are forfeited and the merchandise returns to inventory.Unless a manager overides the decision
  2. Price Lock‑In – The price on the original Sales Order is guaranteed for the 90‑day layaway term even if the factory increases pricing.
  3. Non‑Cancellation – Layaway sales are final. If the customer cancels, payments convert to non‑refundable store credit valid for 12 months.

2. General Terms & Conditions

  1. All‑Sales‑Final – All transactions (regular, sale, clearance, layaway, financed) are final. No cash or credit‑card refunds; eligible returns receive store credit only(RARE).
  2. Price‑on‑Hold Items – A price quote reserves pricing but not inventory. If the factory discontinues an item, Elite will propose a comparable product.
  3. Four‑Week Scheduling – Schedule pick‑up or delivery at least four weeks in advance to guarantee availability.
  4. Packaging Removal – Factory trash removal is a prepaid service; otherwise packaging is left on‑site.
  5. Payment Frequency – Open balances require at least one payment every 30 days. Missed payments may cancel the order and forfeit all deposits.
  6. Free 90‑Day Layaway – No additional charges for layaway held up to 90 days.
  7. Customer Acceptance – Signing a Sales Order, delivery ticket, or submitting payment constitutes agreement to these terms in full.

3. Delivery Policy

  1. Damage Waiver – Elite is not liable for incidental damage to walls, floors, doorways, landscaping, or personal items. Please prepare pathways in advance.
  2. 30‑Minute Arrival Notice – Drivers call/text about 30 minutes before arrival. Missed calls result in a rescheduling and standard redelivery fee.
  3. Schedule Variability – Traffic, weather, or mechanical issues may shift delivery windows; Elite may reschedule at its discretion.
  4. Fit & Access Responsibility – Measure doorways, stairs, and elevators prior to purchase. Original delivery fee is non‑refundable if items do not fit.

4. Financing Disclaimer

  1. Third‑Party Agreements – Financing is administered solely by independent lenders. Applications, approvals, rates, and fees are controlled by those lenders.
  2. Account Servicing – After credit is issued, all billing or payoff questions must be directed to the lender. Elite cannot adjust your financing account.

5. Merchandise Pick‑Up by Customer

  1. Liability Transfer – Ownership transfers to the customer once items are signed out of our dock. Inspect items before loading; hidden damage must be reported within 24 hours.
  2. Assembly Guarantee – Items assembled by Elite staff are guaranteed against workmanship defects for 30 days. Self‑assembly voids this guarantee.

6. Limited Warranty

  1. Manufacturer Coverage – Elite administers warranties exactly as supplied by each brand. Coverage and duration vary.
  2. Validation Procedure – Warranty claims may require photos, serial numbers, or in‑home inspection before approval.
  3. Proof of Purchase – The original printed or digital receipt is mandatory; bank statements alone are insufficient.

7. “As Is” Policy

  1. Condition Disclosure – “As‑Is” items include floor models, scratch‑and‑dent pieces, or returns and are sold with existing defects shown and priced accordingly.
  2. No After‑Sale Service – These items carry zero warranty and cannot be returned or exchanged for any reason.

8. Custom & Special‑Order Policy

  1. Definition – Any item made in a non‑stock color, fabric, size, configuration, or not regularly stocked in our warehouse.
  2. 50 % Deposit – A non‑refundable 50 % deposit is required and orders are transmitted within 24 hours. After that time, no changes or cancellations are possible.
  3. Estimated Arrival – Usual lead time is 2–16 weeks but may extend due to factory workload, shipping, or customs delays.

9. Force Majeure / Supply‑Chain Delays

  1. Scope – Events such as natural disasters, pandemics, strikes, transportation outages, or raw‑material shortages may delay delivery beyond Elite’s control.
  2. Customer Remedy – Orders remain active during such events; cancellation or refund is not permitted unless the manufacturer officially discontinues the item.

10. Right of Refusal / Safety Clause

  1. Unsafe Conditions – Obstructed pathways, aggressive animals, or structural hazards allow the crew to pause or refuse delivery.
  2. Second‑Trip Fee – After hazards are corrected, a standard redelivery fee applies.
  3. No Liability – Elite is not responsible for additional fees arising from unsafe delivery environments.
